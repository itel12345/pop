❓ Adoption FAQ

🐾 What is the adoption process? ▼

First, browse our available puppies and find one that matches your lifestyle. Then contact us via phone or WhatsApp to schedule a meet-and-greet. We'll discuss the puppy's needs, your home environment, and complete a simple adoption application. Once approved, you pay the adoption fee and take your new family member home!

💉 Are the puppies vaccinated? ▼

Absolutely! All our puppies are fully age-appropriately vaccinated, dewormed, and microchipped. Each puppy comes with a health certificate from our licensed veterinarian. We also provide a vaccination record card.

🏠 Is there a home check required? ▼

We do a friendly, informal home check — usually a brief video call or photos of your living space. We just want to ensure the puppy will have a safe, loving environment. It's quick and stress-free!

💰 What does the adoption fee include? ▼

The adoption fee covers vaccinations, microchipping, deworming, health certificate, and a starter kit (food sample, blanket, toy, and care guide). It also supports our rescue efforts for other puppies in need.

🚗 Can you deliver the puppy? ▼

We prefer in-person pickups so you can meet the puppy first. However, for adopters within 100 miles of Portland, we may offer delivery for a small transport fee. Contact us to discuss!

🔄 What if it doesn't work out? ▼

We have a 30-day return policy. If for any reason the adoption isn't a good fit, you can return the puppy to us — no judgment. Your adoption fee (minus a small rehoming processing cost) will be refunded.
