❓ Adoption FAQ

🐾 What is the adoption process? ▼

Browse our puppies, find your match, then contact us via phone or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a meet-and-greet, discuss the puppy's needs, and complete a simple adoption application. Once approved, pay the fee and take your new family member home!

💉 Are the puppies vaccinated? ▼

Absolutely! All puppies are fully age-appropriately vaccinated, dewormed, and microchipped. Each comes with a health certificate and vaccination record card.

🏠 Is there a home check? ▼

We do a friendly, informal home check — usually a brief video call or photos of your space. Quick and stress-free!

💰 What does the fee include? ▼

The fee covers vaccinations, microchipping, deworming, health certificate, and a starter kit (food sample, blanket, toy, care guide).

🔄 What if it doesn't work out? ▼

30-day return policy. If the adoption isn't a good fit, return the puppy — no judgment. Fee refunded minus a small processing cost.
